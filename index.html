Safu Lock DeFi key points

DeFi platform that combines inexpensive solutions for IDOs with high-rewarding tokenomics for day-to-day users.

Simple and Inexpensive DeFi

Safu DeFi is the simple and inexpensive tool designed for those IDOs who are looking to provide trust for investors. Lock your BSC or LP tokens in less than a minute and 5 clicks, then share your locker link (URL) with your community and investors!

Safu Locker

Safu Locker is designed to be impossible to revert. In other words, please double-check your lock inputs (tokens amount and locking period) as not even a DEV will be able to recover them afterwards!
Please be aware that only the locker creator will be able to withdraw the tokens (when unlock date comes, of course), so please make sure that you're using the correct wallet!

SAFU Earn

Safu Lock will reward all the $KEY holders every time someone initiate a transfer event (Ex: Buys or Sells) and will put the rewards directly into holders wallets. It's enough to buy a small bag of $KEY tokens and HODL as much as you can to get more tokens.
